UI Presidential Scholar Nick J. Rolston'sdecision to attend Iowa was inspired by a visit he had with Professor Jack D.Scudderof the UI Physics and Astronomy Department as a high school junior. Three years later, Nick meets with Professor Scudder to discuss that initial encounter, the challenges and successes of scientific research, and the thrill of discovery.
